Meet Debojyoti Chakrabarty

"Growing up in West Bengal, I saw firsthand the untapped potential of skilled workers in smaller cities. QuickBuddy is my solution to bridge this gap and create economic opportunities."

As a self-taught developer and entrepreneur, Debojyoti is passionate about building technology that solves real problems for everyday Indians. QuickBuddy was born from his vision to empower local communities through technology.

The platform is designed specifically for the needs and challenges of Tier 2/3 cities, where traditional service marketplaces often fall short.
